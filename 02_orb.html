HOW TO PLAY
🕹️
Move
Use the joystick (bottom-left) to navigate the rings. On desktop use WASD or arrow keys. Tap-and-hold on the maze to pathfind.
✦
Collect Orbs
Find enough glowing orbs to match the shadow's ring radius. Each orb increases your mass, which slows time — helpful, but the shadow still grows. The target orb count is revealed by the shadow itself.
🌑
The Shadow
A dark shadow expands from the centre outward. It blurs maze walls inside it. When it reaches the limit ring, it's game over.
🏠
Escape
Once all orbs are collected, return to the lighthouse at the centre before the shadow closes in. The clock glows red as time runs low.
⏱️
Time Dilation
Each orb slows your clock. The TIME dial glows red when the shadow is close. Watch the minimap to track orb locations and shadow growth.
