System Override: How to Play

  • The Abacus: The top beads are worth 5 (or 50, 500). Bottom beads are worth 1 (or 10, 100). Tap them to slide them to the glowing purple bar.
  • Numbers Mode: Slide the beads so they equal the falling glowing number.
  • Operations Mode: Keep a running total! If a "+15" falls, add 15 to your current board. If "-8" falls, subtract 8.
  • The Beat: Wait for the falling pulse to hit the bottom rings, then strike the matching L, M, or R button!
