Jin, The Architect

I started this journey as a father who simply wanted to spark my child’s interest in maths. As a graphic designer and illustrator with a deep-seated love for board games, I found myself frustrated by the state of "educational" software—most of which felt like contrived, reskinned exam questions. I knew we could do better. By blending my design background with AI-aided game mechanics, I’ve integrated real board game concepts into the curriculum. The goal? To turn "Dovit’s" struggle into a strategic, beautiful experience where the math is the power-up, not the hurdle.

The world of Dovit and the Monolith is just the beginning. I’m open to collaborating on projects that prioritize genuine engagement over brute memorisation. If you’re interested in these concepts or want to discuss how we might expand this geometric universe together, I’d love to hear from you! Let’s connect and see how we can turn more of these giants into allies.
