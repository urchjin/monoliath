How to Play

Your goal is to guide ABZebra and your team to the Oasis by building grammatically and logically correct sentences!

  • Build a Sentence: The board needs 3 cards: 1 Connector (Purple) and 2 Clauses (Colored).
  • Match the Context: Clause cards must match shapes and colors (e.g., Water 💧 pairs with Water 💧).
  • Cause vs. Effect: The game now strictly reads your sentence. If you put the Cause and Effect in the wrong order, the logic will fail!
  • Watch the Polarity:
    ➡️ Cause (Because, Since): Connects matching polarities (➕ to ➕, or ➖ to ➖).
    🔀 Contrast (Although, While): Connects twisting polarities (➕ to ➖, or ➖ to ➕).
  • No Tautologies: You cannot use the exact same clause twice in one sentence!
  • Sandbox Turns: It's your turn until you hit End Turn ⏭️! Tap a card, then tap a slot to play it. When the board is full, click Check Logic ✨.
