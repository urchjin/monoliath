How to Play

Your goal is to guide ABZebra to the Oasis by building sentences with perfect Subject-Verb Agreement!

  • Build a Sentence: The board needs 3 cards in exact order: [Subject] + [Verb] + [Object].
  • Match the Context: All 3 cards must share the same theme (e.g., Water 💧 pairs with Water 💧).
  • Subject-Verb Agreement: The Subject and the Verb must match in plurality! Watch out for tricky irregular verbs!
  • Watch the Badges:
    1️⃣ Singular: Represents one thing or a singular verb.
    🔢 Plural: Represents multiple things or a plural verb.
    🎯 Any (Object): Objects fit with either singular or plural, as long as the theme matches.
  • Sandbox Turns: It's your turn until you hit End Turn ⏭️! Tap a card, then tap a slot to play it. When the board is full, click Check Grammar ✨.
