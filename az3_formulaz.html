📜 How to Play

  • The Formula: Your speed is:
    Speed = (Coefficient × X_Value) + Constant
  • Bumping: You can land on an occupied space to bump the previous occupant forward!
  • The Junk Deck: You start with 4 Blank cards. Find TRIM DECK cards to permanently trash them!
  • Catch-Up Rule: If an opponent is 10% ahead, you passively gain +2 Constant on your turn (unless that opponent is your teammate!).
  • Playing Cards: Select 1 card to PLAY (grants temp speed). Select up to 2 cards to DISCARD. Discards amplify your PLAY card's stats MASSIVELY for that turn.
  • Math Hazards: Landing on Potholes triggers an Algebra Challenge. Solve it in 3 tries, or lose your next turn! (Trees block the path entirely).
  • Loot Tiles: Land on Dirt, Blue, Purple, Teal, or Red squares to get cards. You have unlimited tries to unlock them!
