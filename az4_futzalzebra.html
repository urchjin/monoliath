How to Play Futzal Zebra

  • Win Condition: First team to score 2 goals wins the match!
  • The Math: The ball (x) starts at 1. When passed to a player, it increases by their formula!
  • Scoring (>15): You can only score if the ball's value is 15 or above.
  • Passing vs Shooting: Use the Mode Button. Passes travel 1 space per turn. Shots travel 2 spaces! Passes into empty space become Loose Balls (x=1).
  • Action Limits: Team WITH the ball can only make 1 run. Team WITHOUT the ball can make 2 runs.
  • Turnovers: Tackles and Interceptions instantly reset the ball to 1!
