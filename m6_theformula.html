THE FORMULA
BODMAS PROTOCOL INITIATED
1. You start with a SYS_VAL of 0.

2. Move onto Number Tiles. You will then choose the Operator to apply (+, -, *, /). It immediately updates your value and adds the resulting token to your inventory.

FIREWALL RULE: You CANNOT step on a Number Tile if your current SYS_VAL is a multiple of its number! (Exception: SYS_VAL 0 can move anywhere). 3. Composite Hulks (Orange): Patrols. Safe ONLY if you share a Common Factor > 1.
WARNING: If you have no common factor AND the Hulk is larger than you, you are teleported back to the start!

4. Factorisers (Purple): Chases you. Divides your value if divisible, else subtracts.
They deactivate for 15 seconds after a successful hit!

5. Collect Brackets from the grid. Reach the EXIT to form an equation matching the TARGET!
