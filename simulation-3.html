HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Outmaneuver opponents on a grid to collect tokens and claim area bonuses. Form complex polygons with your path to earn a higher total score than your opponent.

Controls: Click or tap grid cells to move your pawns. Use the "Claim Area" interface to select vertices on your path and input calculated area values.

How it helps: This game strengthens geometric logic and coordinate geometry skills by challenging players to calculate the areas and interior angles of the polygons they create during gameplay.
